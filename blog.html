30/03/2018 Holding Still Update #11
What was previously known as Ljósálfar now has a proper title: "Holding Still".
Major addition: ACT II has been completed, finally. Now the screenplay is gapless.
'Return' has been compacted and refined. 'Elven cave' as well, I've also added memory loss as a way of having consequences in her action. I personally think it feels fitting in a story about moments, to have moments lost. It's not too sad either as they can make new ones. Basically everything got its tweaks.
Smilla has been renamed to Felinne, for personal reasons and because of the way it sounds and what effect it might have, also because "Notte and Smilla" sounded weird as a couple to me, so I wanted to change that.
Felinne and Notte's ages have been changed to 19 to make Felinne's story believable, therefore they are now students.
