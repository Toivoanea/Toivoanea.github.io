Nea Springer

Gaffer and pastime Screenwriter

Brighton / London | 24 years old

I fell in love with film through the ability to have a positive impact, while also achieving creative and technical fulfilment.

My passion for channelling emotion is both present in my screenwriting and lighting.

I've worked on a variety of forms, but predominantly in narrative and music videos.

My lighting journey has made me develop tastes, I love exercising control over ambient light, but also love looking at a room and finding the quality of light that works for the key given the project's context.

I love following my DPs on the way they see light tightly, bringing our ways together - the best days are the ones on which we're both excited by our light!
