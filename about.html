Leon Springer

Gaffer and pastime Screenwriter

Brighton / London | 23 years old

I fell in love with film through the ability to entertain and make a change in the world, as well as the creative and technical fulfilment.

My passion for channelling emotion is both present in my screenwriting and lighting.

At the heart of filmmaking lies technique; the necessary skill to work creatively. I'm avidly learning the electrical and physical properties of lighting.

I've worked on a variety of forms, but predominantly in narrative and music videos. In narrative work I consistently use motivated light in a naturalistic or stylistic way.

Music videos are great for exploring ideas quickly and often expressionistically, following genre conventions or juxtaposing them with their opposite.
