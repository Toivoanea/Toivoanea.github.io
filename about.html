Linnea Springer

Gaffer and pastime Screenwriter

Brighton / London | 23 years old

I fell in love with film through the ability to have a positive impact, while also achieving creative and technical fulfilment.

My passion for channelling emotion is both present in my screenwriting and lighting.

I've worked on a variety of forms, but predominantly in narrative and music videos. In narrative work I'm drawn to a motivated light approach, but not exclusively in a naturalistic way.

I love Music videos as they're a great for exploring ideas quickly and often expressionistically, following genre conventions or opposing them boldly.
