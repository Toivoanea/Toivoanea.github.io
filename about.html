Leon Springer

Aspiring Screenwriter and Gaffer

Brighton / London | 22 years old

I fell in love with film because of the medium's ability to entertain and change the world.

Especially, the inner world of a character or place is important to me which is why I started filmmaking with a passion for screenwriting. This passion translates into lighting which to me is always tied to the story or in the case of non-narrative content to the emotional truth of the piece. To me, though at the heart of filmmaking lies technique which one has to master to work more creatively.

My passions for programming and sciences have translated into a passion for technical details in lighting where I'm avidly learning the electrical and physical properties of lighting and light to get the fullest understanding of how lighting works.

I've worked on a variety of forms though predominantly in narrative and music videos. For narrative work I've always followed an approach of motivated lighting and I love both naturalistic and stylistic approaches; whatever fits the story.

Though I also like music videos which are a fun way of exploring ideas quickly and sometimes expressionistically, following all kinds of genres and sometimes juxtaposing them with their opposite.

I see myself continuing this trajectory of shorts and music videos, though with occasional explorations into other areas. Eventually, planning to climb the ladder in independent features.
