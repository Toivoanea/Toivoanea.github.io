Leon Springer

Aspiring Screenwriter and Gaffer

Brighton / London | 22 years old

Feel free to contact at LeonSpringerCom@gmail.com

I first fell in love with film because of the ability to entertain and make a change in the world.

The emotional world of a character or place is especially important to me which is why I started filmmaking with a passion for screenwriting. This passion translates into lighting which to me is always tied to story. Even in the case of non-narrative content I like to work with the emotional truth of the piece. To me, though at the heart of filmmaking lies technique serving as the tool to work more creatively.

My passions for programming and sciences have translated into a passion for technical details in lighting where I'm avidly learning the electrical and physical properties of lighting and light to get the fullest understanding of lighting.

I've worked on a variety of forms though predominantly in narrative and music videos. For narrative work I always follow an approach of motivated lighting and I love both naturalistic and stylistic approaches; whatever fits the story.

To me, music videos are a fun way of exploring ideas quickly and sometimes expressionistically, following all kinds of genres and sometimes juxtaposing them with their opposite.

I see myself continuing this trajectory of shorts and music videos, though with occasional explorations into other areas. Eventually, planning to hone my craft in independent features.
